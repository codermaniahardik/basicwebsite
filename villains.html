Green Goblin

The Green Goblin is the alter ego of Norman Osborn, a wealthy industrialist and scientist who becomes insane after being exposed to experimental performance-enhancing chemicals.The Green Goblin possesses superhuman strength, enhanced agility, and durability due to the effects of the Goblin Serum. Compared to a normal human being, Green Goblin is significantly stronger and more resilient.

Kingpin

Kingpin is a major antagonist in the Spider-Man and Punisher franchises and the main antagonist in the Daredevil franchise. He is initially portrayed as a crafty crime lord in Spider-Man's rogues' gallery, but later becomes Daredevil's archenemy. In the MCU, Kingpin is always looking for the perfect opportunity to assert his dominance. He finally gets his chance after the Battle of New York leaves the city in ruins. Kingpin is also known as the biggest and most influential crime lord in Gotham City and one of Batman's greatest enemies.

Doctor Octopus

Doctor Octopus is a brilliant engineer, inventor, and the world's leading authority on nuclear radiation. He is also a highly intelligent and prideful scientist. During an atomic research project, Doctor Octopus' body became fused to four mechanical, tentacle-like arms. This caused him severe brain damage and turned him into a criminal. Without his artificial tentacles, Doctor Octopus has normal human strength.

Mysterio

He is a master illusionist who uses his Hollywood special effects training to create mind-bending visions. Mysterio's real name is Quentin Beck, a former actor and special effects artist who seeks to become famous by defeating Spider-Man.

Carnage

The Carnage symbiote can give its host an elongated jaw, fangs, claws, tentacles, and prehensile tongue. The Cletus Kasady incarnation of Carnage has appeared in several games, including The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (1992), Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade's Revenge, and Spider-Man and Venom: Maximum Carnage
