Heros



Spider-Man, Peter Parker, a poor and sickly orphan, is bitten by a radioactive spider and gains superhuman strength, speed, and agility. He also gains the ability to cling to walls. Peter Parker originally uses his powers for personal gain, but after his Uncle Ben is killed by a thief, he begins to use his powers to fight crime. He uses his powers to help others while also dealing with personal obstacles.
