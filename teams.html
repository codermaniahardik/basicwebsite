Web-Warriors

the Web Warriors are a small group of heroes from across the multiverse. They protect areas without Spider-Men after the Inheritors' actions. The Web Warriors are formed from the remains of the "Spider-Army" that defeated Morlun and are all connected to Spider-Man in some way.

Avengers

The Avengers (of Earth-616) is a team of superheroes that often band together to fight the threats that no one hero can handle alone.

Frighful Four

The Frighful Four (of Earth-616) was a supervillain team founded by the Wizard, Trapster, and Sandman dedicated to humiliating the Fantastic Four by becoming an evil, mirror counterpart.

Sinister Six

The Sinister Six (of Earth-616) are a group comprised of villains who have each attempted to kill Spider-Man on numerous occasions who have united in order to exact vengeance on their mutual foe. The group was originally comprised of members of Spider-Man's initial rogues gallery, but the team's roster has expanded over the years to include many prominent Spider-Man villains including the Rhino, Venom and the Hobgoblin.
